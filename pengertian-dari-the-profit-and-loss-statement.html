11. Mr. bayu buys a car at a price of $5000 because the car is broken he sells it getting a price of $8000 does he suffer a profit of a loss? determine the percentage of his profit or lossjangan jawab asal asal bg​


Jawab:

Mr. Bayu bought a car for $5000 and sold it for $8000. To determine if he suffered a profit or loss, we need to calculate the difference between the selling price and the buying price:

Profit/Loss = Selling price - Buying price

Profit/Loss = $8000 - $5000

Profit/Loss = $3000

Since the difference is positive, Mr. Bayu made a profit of $3000.

To determine the percentage of his profit or loss, we need to divide the profit by the buying price and multiply by 100:

Percentage Profit/Loss = (Profit/Loss ÷ Buying price) x 100%

Percentage Profit/Loss = ($3000 ÷ $5000) x 100%

Percentage Profit/Loss = 60%

Therefore, Mr. Bayu made a profit of 60% on the sale of the car.

Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that
Raju purchased a dozen pens ($0.5/unit)
A dozen = 12 units
$0.5 × 12 units = $6
-> purchasing cost (pc) = $6
-> selling cost (sc) = $9

Raju gets profit, sc > pc
Find the percentage of profit (%)

% = 100(sc-pc)/pc
% = 100(9-6)/6
% = 100(3)/6
% = 300/6
% = 50 (%)

Raju gets 50% of profit

24. ada yang tau gakpengertian dari financial loss dan non financial loss​


Bismillah

Jawaban Serta Penjelasan:

Financial loss dan non financial loss​ adalah bahasa Inggris yang artinya

1. Financial loss = Kerugian / risiko finansial

2. non Financial loss = Kerugian / risiko non finansial

Sedangkan pengertiannya adalah

1. Financial loss / Kerugian / risiko finansial adalah resiko yang diderita oleh investor sebagai akibat dari ketidakmampuan emiten saham dan obligasi memenuhi kewajiban pembayaran deviden atau bunga atau bunga serta pokok pinjaman.

2. non Financial loss / Kerugian / risiko non finansial adalah risiko yang dampak kerugiannya tidak dapat dinilai atau diukur secara keuangan, contohnya risiko kesalahan dalam memilih karir dan risiko kesalahan dalam memilih pasangan hidup.

Reported Speech, dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya adalah kalimat tidak langsung. Perubahan kalimat dari Direct Speech menjadi Reported Speech memiliki beberapa aturan.  Ini adalah beberapa contoh:

1) imperative speech

My mother said, "don't open that door!"

my mother said not to open that door.

Imperative: “Come here.” Reported Speech: She told me to come here. So, we use “ask/tell + to + verb” to report a command or imperative.

2) present tense

She said, "She is a dancer."

She said (that) She was a dancer.

Jika kalimat ini berbentuk kebenaran umum, maka akan lebih sesuai  

She said (that) She is a dancer.

3) past tense

my mother said, "You brother did his homework last night."

my mother said (that) my brother had done his homework previous night.

4) Present Perfect Tense

He asked me,"have you been to spain?"

He asked me if I had been to spain.

5) Modals

You asked me, "Can you come?"

You asked me if / whether I could come.

Kata-kata (that) setelah kata said that boleh dipakai atau dihilangkan saja.

Cost Price = $444.48 / (1 - 0.074)
