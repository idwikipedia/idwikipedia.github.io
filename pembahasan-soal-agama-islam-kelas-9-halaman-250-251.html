19. Soal ¹/16+⅛+¼+........+16=x Nilai x adalah A.511/16 B.251/16 C.511/8 D.251/8 E.127/4 Beserta rumusnya


U1 = 1/16
Un = 16
r = U2/U1 = 1/8 : 1/16 = 1/8 × 16 = 16/8 = 2

Un = U1 × r^(n - 1)
16 = 1/16 × 2^(n - 1)
16 × 16 = 2^(n - 1)
2^4 × 2^4 = 2^(n - 1)
2^(4 + 4) = 2^(n - 1)
2^8 = 2^(n - 1)(2 habis, tinggal pangkatnya saja)
8 = n - 1
n = 8 + 1 = 9

S9 = a[(r^n) - 1]/(r - 1)
= 1/16 [(2^9) - 1]/(2 - 1)
= 1/16 (512 - 1)/1
= 1/16 × 511 = 511/16 (A)

^ = pangkat
