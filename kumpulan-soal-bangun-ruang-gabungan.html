3. tolong k pakek cara ya mo di kumpulanitu soalnya ada 2 yaa-volume gabungan bangun ruang-luas permukaan gabungan bangun ruang​


Jawaban:

-Tabung

Dik : d = 14cm, r = 7cm

t =12 cm

Dit : Volume, Luas permukaan

Jawab:

1. Volume tabung

[tex]\pi {r}^{2} t \\ = \frac{22}{7} \times {7}^{2} \times 12 \\ = 22 \times 7 \times 12 \\ = 1848 {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Luas permukaan

[tex]2\pi \: r(r + t) \\ = 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 7(7 + 12) \\ = 44 \times 19 \\ = 836 {cm}^{2} [/tex]

- Kerucut

Dik : r = 7cm

t = 18-12 = 6cm

Dit: Volume,Luas

Jawab:

Volume

[tex] \frac{1}{3} \pi {r}^{2} t \\ = \frac{1}{ 3} \times \frac{22}{7} \times {7}^{2} \times 6 \\ = 22 \times 7 \times 2 \\ = 924 {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Selimut( Untuk mencari Luas permukaan)

[tex] \sqrt{ {r}^{2} + {t}^{2} } \\ = \sqrt{ {7}^{2} + {6}^{2} } \\ = \sqrt{49 + 36} \\ = \sqrt{85} \\ = 5 \sqrt{3.4} [/tex]

Luas permukaan

[tex]\pi {r}^{} (r + s) \\ = \frac{22}{7} \times 7(5 \sqrt{3.4} + 7) \\ = 22 \times 12 \sqrt{3.4} \\ = 264 \sqrt{3.4} \\ = 486.79 {cm}^{2} [/tex]

Dijumlahkan

Volume

1848 + 924

= 2772cm³.

Luas permukaan

836 + 486,79

= 1322,79cm³.
