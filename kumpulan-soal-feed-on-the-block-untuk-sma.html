3. ( +) Paula usually feeds her rabbits on the morning. The correct interrogative sentence is .. A. Is Paula feeds her rabbits on the morning? B. Has Paula feed her rabbits on the morning ? C. Did Paula feed her rabbits on the morning? D. Does Paula feed her rabbits on the morning?


The correct interrogative sentence is Does Paula feed her rabbits on the morning?

Jawabannya adalah D ✔️

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, rutin, kebiasaan dan fakta.

Ciri Present Tense

=> usually ✔️

=> always

=> Every day

=> twice a week

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es)

(-) S + do/does + not + V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Berdasarkan Rumus Present Tense

(+) Paula usually feeds her rabbits on the morning.

(-) Paula does not usually feed her rabbits on the morning.

(?) Does Paula feed her rabbits on the morning?

KESIMPULAN

The correct interrogative sentence is D.

Keterangan:

➡️Kalimat tanya yang benar adalah yang D

➡️ karena menggunakan DOES sebagai Auxiliary

A. Is Paula feeds her rabbits on the morning?❌B. Has Paula feed her rabbits on the morning ?❌C. Did Paula feed her rabbits on the morning?❌D. Does Paula feed her rabbits on the morning?✔️

28. The zookeeper always...... The animals at the zoo. A. To feed B. Feed C. Feeds D. Feeding


PART = SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE-UNDERSTANDING THE CONTEXT FROM THE TEXT

-------------------------------

Jawaban:

C. feeds

Note :

Struktur kalimat diatas menerapkan pola simple present tense bentuk verbal. Bisa dicermati pada penggunaan adverb of frequency "always" yang menjelaskan habit/kebiasaan/kejadian yang terus berulang-ulang.

Penjelasan:

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSEmerupakan tenses yang digunakan mendeskripsikan kejadian nyata di masa lampau, saat ini dan akan tetap selalu sama  di tahun - tahun mendatang.Tenses ini juga menjabarkan fakta kebenaran umum, kebenaran yang tak terbatas dan aktivitas yang terus- menerus berulang  ( kebiasaan/habit/ hobby )Penggunaan keterangan frekuensi ( Adverb of frequency) yang menjadi ciri khas utama dalam menyatakan habit/kebiasaan dalam simple present tense  :

Always, usually, often, everyday, seldom, rarely, daily, never, frequently, occasionally , ever, sometimes, regularly.

PATTERN

Struktur kalimat dikategorikan ke dalam 2 jenis, Verbal dan nominal.

1. Rumus Simple present tense ( verbal sentence )

Verbal sentence merupakan kalimat yang predikatnya berupa kata kerja aksi/tindakan.

(+) SUBJECT + VERB (S/ES ) + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT

(-)  SUBJECT + DO/DOES +NOT + VERB 1 + OBJECT /COMPLEMENT

(?) DO/DOES+SUBJECT + VERB 1 + OBJECT / COMPLEMENT?

Example :

(+ ) Mrs.Dew always reads the Vogue magazine   ( Habit/kebiasaan/ hobby)

( - ) Mrs. Dew  doesn't always read the Vogue magazine .

(? ) Does Mrs. Dew always  read the Vogue magazine?

2. Rumus simple present tense ( nominal sentence )

Nominal sentence merupakan kalimat yang predikatnya bukan berupa kata kerja aksi. Pada umumnya berupa kata benda ( noun), kata sifat ( adjective ), dan keterangan ( adverb )

(+ ) SUBJECT + TOBE (IS/AM/ARE)+NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB

(- ) SUBJECT + TOBE(IS/AM/ARE ) + NOT + NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB

( ? ) TOBE(IS/AM/ARE ) + SUBJECT+NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB?

Example :

( + ) Mrs. Dew is a diligent woman.  ( penjabaran karakter, bagian sifat )

( - ) Mrs. Dew is not a diligent woman.

( ? ) Is mrs.Dew a diligent woman ?

29. 3. A block of mass m/ = 4.0 kg is put on top of a block of mass m2 = 5.0 kg. To cause the top block to slip on the bottom one while the bottom one is held fixed, a horizontal force of at least 12 N must be applied to the top block. The blocks assembly is now placed on a horizontal, frictionless table, as shown in FIGURE 1. Find the magnitudes of a.the maximum horizontal force that can be applied to the lower block so that the blocks will move together b.the resulting acceleration of the blocks.


The maximum horizontal force that can be applied to the lower block is 5 N.The resulting acceleration of the blocks are 21,1 m/s².

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui:

Massa 1 = 4 kg

Berat 1 = 40 N

Massa 2 = 5 kg

Berat 2 = 50 N

F1 = 12 N

Ditanya:

Gaya pada blok paling bawah (F2)?Percepatan (a)?

Jawab:

∑Fy1 = 0

N1 - W cos θ = 0

12 - 120 cos θ = 0

12 = 120 c0s θ

cos θ = 0,1

∑Fx1 = m1 x a

120 - T = 4a....(1)

∑Fy2 = 0

N2 - w cos θ = 0

N2 = w cos θ

N2 = 50 (0,1) = 5 N

∑Fx2 = m2 x a

50 sin θ + T = 5a

50 x 1 + T = 5a

50 + T = 5a..........(2)

120 - T = 4a

50 + T = 5a

___________ +

190 = 9a

a = 21,1 m/s²

from that calculation, we know that:

The maximum horizontal force that can be applied to the lower block is 5 N.The resulting acceleration of the blocks are 21,1 m/s².

32. A 10 kg block with an initial velocity of 10 m/s slides 10 meters across a horizontal surface and comes to a stop. It takes the block 2 seconds to stop. What is the stopping force acting on the block?


Jawaban:

F = 50 N

Penjelasan:

m = 10 kg

v0 = 10 m/s

vt = 0 m/s

Δx = 10 m

t = 2 s

vt = v0 + at

0 = 10 + 2a

-2a = 10

a = -5 m/s²

F = m x a

F = 10 x 5

F = 50 N, with opposite direction

37. A student is investigating the pressure that a block of wood is exerting on the floor. The weight of the wood is 20 N. The block is 10 cm long, 5 cm wide, and 2 cm high. a What is the biggest pressure that the block can exert on the floor? b What is the smallest pressure that the block can exert on the floor?


Jawaban:
a. 200 Pa ( Pascal )
b. 40 Pa ( Pascal )

Penjelasan: Well, because the question asked for maximum and minimum pressure,
then we have to find the area of ​​the largest and smallest contact area, on a wooden block there are three variations of the area of ​​​​the touch area as follows:

Area Of Touch 1 :
→ A¹ = L x W = 10 x 5 = 50cm = 0,5 m

Area Of Touch 2 :
→ A² = L x h = 10 x 2 = 20cm = 0,2m

Area of touch 3 :
→ A³ = W X h = 5 x 2 = 10cm = 0,1 m

Of the three variations of the touch area above, the largest and smallest touch areas are A¹ and A²


Then the maximum pressure is:
→ Pmax = F/A³ = (w x g)/A³
Pmax = 20/0,1 = 200

Then the minimum pressure is:
→ Pmin = F/A¹ = (w x g)/A¹
Pmin = 20/0,5 = 40
