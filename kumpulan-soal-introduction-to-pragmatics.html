Explication

Cooperative principle adalah teori tentang cara mencapai suatu komunikasi yang efektif, bagaimana menghindari kesalahpahaman dalam suatu komunikasi akibat penyampaian informasi yang kurang tepat.

Grice's maxims of conversation adalah maksim-maksim (kebenaran umum tentang sifat manusia) yang dicetuskan oleh ahli sosiologi dan linguistik Paul Grice tentang prinsip-prinsip yang secara naluriah diikuti oleh orang-orang dalam percakapan.

Ada empat maksim utama dalam percakapan menurut Grice, yaitu:

maxim of quantity → seinformatif mungkin, dan memberi informasi secukupnya, tidak berlebih.maxim of quality → jujur, tidak memberi pernyataan yang kamu percayai tidak benar, tidak memberi pernyataan yang tidak didukung oleh bukti yang memadai.maxim of relation/relevance → memberi informasi yang relevan atau nyambung dengan pembicaraan.maxim of manner → memberi pernyataan/informasi yang jelas, tidak menggunakan kata-kata/bahasa yang sulit dimengerti, tidak ambigu, padat, dan runtut sehingga mudah dimengerti lawan bicara.

13. why must discourse and pragmatics? why not discourse and semantics? ​


Why does it have to be discourse and pragmatics?

why not discourse and semantics?

-----------------------

Answer

Because discourse and pragmatics deal with meaning in context.

-----------------------

Explanation

In linguistics, discourse refers to the study of verbal or written language in a communicative context. Discourse is the way language is used to convey its meaning. This meaning is dictated by the social conditions, the speaker of the language and the conditions at the time it is spoken.

Semantics in linguistics is the study of words and their meanings.

Pragmatics in linguistics is also the study of words and their meanings but with emphasize in the context in which they are used.

Semantics focuses on the literal meaning of the words while pragmatics focuses on the literal and inferred meanings and also the context, such as deixis, turns in the conversation, and text organisation.

So it can be concluded that why discourse is more intertwined with pragmatics than with semantics because both discourse and pragmatics deal with meaning in context, not just the literal meaning.
