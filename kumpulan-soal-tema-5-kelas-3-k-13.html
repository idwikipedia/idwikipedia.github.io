27. nilai k pada soal 4/5+13/4-8/10=k

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex] \frac{4}{5} + \frac{13}{4} - \frac{8}{10} \\ \frac{4}{5} \times \frac{4}{4} + \frac{13}{4} \times \frac{5}{5} - \frac{8}{10} \times \frac{2}{2} \\ \frac{16}{20} + \frac{65}{20} - \frac{16}{20} \\ \frac{65}{20} = 3 \frac{5}{20} = 3 \frac{1}{4} [/tex]
